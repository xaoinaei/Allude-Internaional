About us

Allude International school

(Preschool to K5)

We are India’s Leading preschool brand going to expand up to K5 operating as the preschool segment of Allude International School with the prime focus of transforming the nature of learning in an early childhood environment. Allude preschool is recognized as a developmentally enriching space, making learning fun for 1.8 to 5 years. We thrive hard to shape the future of the young ones as they are the leaders of tomorrow.

01

Why Choose us

Allude Education aims at redefining the early year learning space in India. It is known for its “Child-Centric philosophy” and time-tested curriculum that focuses on the holistic development of the child through cutting-edge technological resources. Enduring its key attributes to provide value education to every child and to create an environment that supports holistic development, Allude ventured into the primary school segment with the vision to create a system that is highly observed for its academic excellence and endeavors to raise socially cognizant and responsible individuals. Now we are going to expand the preschool segment up to class 5. Allude Education aims to work for improving student outcome and efficiency through better learning.

02

Our Mission

Allude education aims to provide high-quality, value-based education with holistic child development; in a safe and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for lifelong learning.

03

Our vision

Allude education envisions to developing an excellent learning environment, to provide affordable, accessible and high-quality education across Nation. Allude’s students centric, activity-based pedagogy strives to nurture life skills, resilience, and confidence to enable every child in achieving their unique potential to the fullest.
